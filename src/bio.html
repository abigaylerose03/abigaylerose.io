"Chic and Geek"

Hi there! My name is Abby and I am currently a highschool student with a big aspiration in life.
To become a software engineer.
Before I was 10 years old, coding didn't exist until my younger sister started learning Processing.js on Khan Academy. I thought that was the coolest thing ever..typing random strings of text on your keyboard and making something appear out of thin air. Computer programming seemed like magic!
Since then, I started immersing myself to a bunch of different websites that taught me how to code. My first programming language I ever learned was Ruby on Codecademy. Afterwards, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, etc. The list goes on and on.
To any of you out there trying to learn how to code or looking for a future career in the tech industry, I highly suggest learning how to code! It's extremely empowering because of the ability to work from anywhere, the endless possibilities of creating with the art of computer languages, and the potential to make true, tangible impact on the globe from wherever your laptop goes. Now, that's magic.
I've been coding for 6 years and counting, the grind doesn't stop until you do.
