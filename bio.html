Since then, I started immersing myself to a bunch of different websites that taught me how to code. My first programming language I ever learned was Python on Codecademy. Afterwards, Ruby, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, MySQL, Java, etc.
A job in the tech industry empowers the lives of others, as well as your own, to manifest a rewarding experience. The endless possibilities of creating with the art of computer languages, and the potential to make true, tangible impact on the globe from wherever your laptop goes, now, that's magic.
I've been coding for 6 years and counting, the grind doesn't stop until you do.
