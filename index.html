About Me

Hello! My name is Abigayle Peterson.
I'm a freshman at Western Washington University in the honors college and Computer Science/Math Scholars program funded by the National Science Foundation. My ultimate goal in life is to inspire, highlight, and support minorities in tech. By doing so, we can help everyone feel like they have a place in the STEM world.

I am the founder of Magnify, an upcoming free mental health app. Magnify's mission is to provide free, high-quality mental health services to ensure everyone gains equal access to wellness support. My aspiration for this app is to incorporate artificial intelligence to automate the experience of gaining affordable care in the industry's health sectors.

Outside of coding, I love to write for Reinvented Magazine, read historical fiction novels, hike with friends, listen to catchy rap music, and fuel my love for fancy lattes.

Feel free to connect to me via email! I'm always open to new perspectives, internship opportunities, and project collaborations.
