About Me

Hello! My name is Abigayle Peterson.
I'm based in Greater Seattle Area and am an aspiring software engineer. My ultimate goal in life is to inspire, highlight, and support underrepresented groups in the tech. By doing so, we can help everyone feel like they have a place in the STEM world.

I am the founder of Magnify, a mental-wellness app initiative that provides encouragement, access to health resources, and an emphasis in daily mindful journaling. My aspiration for this app is to incorporate artificial intelligence to automate the experience of gaining affordable care in the industry's health sectors.

Outside of coding, I love to write for Reinvented Magazine, read historical fiction novels, hike with friends, listen to catchy rap music, and fuel my love for fancy lattes.

Feel free to connect to me via email! I'm always open to new perspectives, internship opportunities, and project collaborations.
